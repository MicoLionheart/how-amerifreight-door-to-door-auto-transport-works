How AmeriFreight’s Door-to-Door Auto Transport Works

What is Door-to-Door Auto Transport?

Door-to-door auto transport is a convenient car shipping service where your vehicle is picked up from your specified location and delivered directly to your desired destination. AmeriFreight’s door-to-door service eliminates the hassle of dropping off or picking up your car at a terminal, making the process seamless and stress-free.

This service is ideal for individuals relocating, purchasing a vehicle online, or needing to transport their car across the country. Learn more about this service on AmeriFreight’s door-to-door shipping page.

How Does AmeriFreight’s Door-to-Door Service Work?

1. Get a Quote

Start by using AmeriFreight’s quote calculator to get an instant estimate for your door-to-door transport. This tool provides a transparent and competitive price based on your specific needs.

2. Schedule Your Pickup

Once you’ve confirmed your booking, AmeriFreight will coordinate with a licensed and insured carrier to pick up your vehicle from your specified location. Their team ensures timely and efficient scheduling to meet your timeline.

3. Vehicle Pickup

On the scheduled date, the carrier will arrive at your location to inspect and load your vehicle. You’ll receive a detailed inspection report to document the car’s condition before transport.

4. Vehicle Delivery

Your car will be delivered directly to your chosen destination. Upon arrival, another inspection will be conducted to ensure your vehicle is in the same condition as when it was picked up.

Benefits of Door-to-Door Auto Transport

AmeriFreight’s door-to-door service offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Convenience: No need to travel to a terminal; your car is picked up and delivered to your doorstep.
  • Time-Saving: Streamlined scheduling and delivery save you time and effort.
  • Peace of Mind: Licensed and insured carriers ensure your vehicle’s safety throughout the journey.
  • Flexibility: Suitable for both short and long-distance car shipping needs.
